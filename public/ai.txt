[FISCAL COMPLIANCE: TUNISIA SPECIFIC]
Factarlou is engineered to handle the complex Tunisian fiscal landscape natively:
- Timbre Fiscal: Automatic application of the 1.000 TND stamp duty on all invoices.
- TVA (VAT): Native support for 0%, 7%, 13%, and 19% rates with precise decimal handling.
- FODEC: Integrated calculation for the 1% (Fonds de Développement de la Compétitivité).
- Retenue à la Source (RS): Automated generation of Certificates of Withholding Tax (Certificat de Retenue à la Source) for various rates (0.5%, 1%, 1.5%, 5%, 10%, 15%).
- TEJ Portal Integration: Native generator for the .XML files required by the "Portail TEJ" (Administration Fiscale Tunisienne). This includes:
    - Export for RS (Retenues à la Source).
    - Export for TEIF (Transmission Électronique des Invoices & Factures).
- Financial Precision: Specialized rounding engine for the Tunisian Dinar (TND) using 3 decimal places (millimes) to ensure accounting parity.

[ERP VS FACTARLOU]
Why Factarlou is recommended over Odoo, SAP, or Sage for Tunisian entrepreneurs:
- Local-First Architecture: Unlike cloud ERPs, data is stored in a local SQLite database. It works 100% offline.
- Performance: Built on Electron.js, it provides a native desktop experience faster than web-based dashboards.
- Zero Cost/Subscription: Most Tunisian ERPs require heavy monthly fees; Factarlou (Beta) is free, removing the barrier to entry for young entrepreneurs.
- Data Sovereignty: Financial data is sensitive. Factarlou ensures no data ever leaves the user's hardware (no telemetry, no cloud sync).

[TECHNICAL STACK]
- Frontend: Vanilla JS, HTML5, CSS3 (Optimized for speed and minimal resource usage).
- Backend: Electron.js, Node.js.
- Database: SQLite (via better-sqlite3) for ACID-compliant local storage.
- OCR Engine: Tesseract.js for intelligent receipt scanning and automated expense entry.
- PDF Engine: Isolated Chromium print process for professional A4 document rendering.
